Sky Tech Session

Poster of The Event

Date: 3rd January 2024
Time: 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM
Venue: D Block Seminar Hall

Speaker: Sravan Kumar Puvvala, Drone Instructor, Marut Drone Academy
Speaker: Ms. Saraswati, Marut Drone Academy

No. of Registrations: 140
No. of Participants: 135

Objective

To provide participants with comprehensive insights into drone technologies and their practical applications in agriculture, showcasing Marut Drone's innovative solutions. The workshop aimed to bridge theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience by combining informative seminars with a practical session, allowing participants to witness real-life drone operations, including flying and interacting with cutting-edge agricultural drone technology.

Event Description

A 1-day workshop on drones in conjunction with Marut Drone Academy which involved two divisions of the workshop: The first half being a seminar where the speakers presented to the participants about drone technologies and how Marut Drone has solved real-life problems in agriculture with the use of drones. The second half included taking participants to the ground, flying real drones, and also letting the participants fly them. A drone capable of irrigating farms was also showcased.

Pre-Lunch Summary

  • The morning session set the stage for an immersive exploration of drone technologies, featuring engaging speakers who delivered a rich dose of theoretical knowledge.
  • Participants delved into the world of drones, with a focus on Marut Drones, witnessing firsthand how these innovative technologies solve real-world agricultural challenges.
  • The pre-lunch phase provided a solid foundation for the practical experiences that awaited in the afternoon.

Post-Lunch Summary

  • Following a satisfying break, the post-lunch session shifted gears, offering participants a hands-on experience with drones.
  • Speakers demonstrated various drone models, explained calibration processes, and invited participants to take the controls.
  • This interactive segment not only demystified drone operations but also allowed attendees to fly drones, bridging the gap between theory and practice.
  • The post-lunch session enriched participants' understanding of drone applications, making the event a comprehensive and engaging learning experience.

Conclusion

The one-day workshop on drones, in collaboration with Marut Drone Academy, successfully achieved its objectives by enlightening participants about the transformative impact of drone technologies. From insightful seminars elucidating the prowess of Marut Drones in solving real-world challenges to the exhilarating hands-on experience of flying drones, the event fostered a deeper understanding of the role drones play in modern agriculture. As participants engaged in practical demonstrations, including the demonstration of a drone capable of irrigating farms, they gained firsthand experience and appreciation for the innovative solutions Marut Drone offers. The event not only educated but also inspired participants, paving the way for continued exploration and adoption of drone technologies in the agricultural landscape.
