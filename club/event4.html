Drone Hack

Organized by Department of Computer Science Engineering, AU

Event Details

Date: 28th June 2024 – 30th June 2024

Time: 09:00 AM – 04:00 PM

Venue: A Block-306

No. of Participants: 70

Objective

Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application in drones. We aim to empower innovators, engineers, and problem-solvers with hands-on experience and industry-relevant skills essential in today's world.

Event Description

Drone Hack is an event with a one-day boot camp and a 36-hour hackathon at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Anurag University. It helps students transform enthusiasm and ideas into working prototypes.

Boot Camp Sessions

Day 1 Summary

Day 1 featured an introduction to drones, starting with vision and mission building. Participants identified opportunities, brainstormed ideas, and aligned them to their goals.

Day 2 Summary

Participants identified problem statements, assembled drones, integrated components, and calibrated accelerometers. By the end of the day, they had working prototypes ready for testing.

Day 3 Summary

Final day included compass calibration, radio setup, and presentations. Teams showcased projects demonstrating how their drones solved real-world problems.

At a Glance

  • Day 1: Basics of UAVs – 3 hours
  • Day 2: Assembling UAV (3 hours), Drone Calibration & Mentoring
  • Day 3: Project Demonstration & Feedback

Outcomes

  1. Participants gained a strong foundation in drone technology.
  2. Teams converged on practical, innovative ideas.
  3. Built first versions of projects with expert feedback.
  4. Learned prototype building and real-time testing.
  5. Top teams received further mentoring opportunities.

Conclusion

The 36-hour event gave participants both knowledge and hands-on skills. They assembled drones, solved problem statements, and showcased innovative projects. The event enhanced teamwork, creativity, and technical expertise—preparing students for real-world drone applications.
