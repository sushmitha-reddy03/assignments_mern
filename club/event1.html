Report on “Drone Workshop”

Poster

Event Details

Date: 30th July 2024

Time: 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM

Venue: D Block Seminar Hall

Speakers: Team “We Got Rizz”, E. Kishan Karthik

No. of Registrations: 120   |   No. of Participants: 75

Objective

To provide participants with comprehensive insights into drone technologies and their practical applications in agriculture, showcasing Wegrotis and Drone Club drones. The workshop aimed to bridge theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience by combining informative seminars with a practical session, allowing participants to witness real-life drone operations, including flying and interacting with drone technology.

Event Description

A 1-day workshop on drones in conjunction with ER, which involved two divisions: the first half was a seminar where the speakers presented on drone technologies and how drones solve real-world problems across sectors. The second half included practical sessions where participants flew real drones, including a demonstration at the cricket ground.

Pre-Lunch Summary

The morning session set the stage for an immersive exploration of drone technologies, featuring engaging speakers who delivered a rich dose of theoretical knowledge. Participants witnessed how innovative technologies solve real-world challenges, providing a solid foundation for the afternoon’s practical sessions.

Snaps from Pre-Lunch Session

Post-Lunch Summary

Following the break, participants engaged in hands-on drone experiences. Speakers demonstrated drone models, explained calibration processes, and invited participants to fly drones themselves. This interactive segment bridged the gap between theory and practice, enriching their understanding of drone applications.

Snaps from Post-Lunch Session

Conclusion

The one-day workshop on drones, in collaboration with “We Got Rizz” team (winners of Tech Hack #3), successfully achieved its objectives by enlightening participants about the transformative impact of drone technologies. From insightful seminars to hands-on flying, the event fostered a deeper understanding of drones in agriculture and other sectors. Participants also engaged in a quiz, making the event both educational and interactive. The workshop inspired continued exploration and adoption of drone technologies in real-world applications.
